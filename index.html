Coin Sounds

This tool helps you celebrate when a coin sells.

Input your PT url including 'http://' or 'https://' and the port if needed, don't include a slash at the end of the URL. server.api_token can be found in your application.properties file.

To change the music or celebration image, replace the .mp3 and .png files with whatever you want. I suggest using a square image to avoid squishing.

Happy trades!
